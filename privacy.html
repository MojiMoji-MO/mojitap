🔒 Privacy Policy

Mojitap (hereinafter 'Company') considers your privacy and data security a top priority. This policy provides transparent information on how your data is handled when using our services.

1. Zero Text Data Collection (100% Local Processing)

Most importantly, no text entered by the user for emoji conversion is ever transmitted to or stored on our servers. All context analysis and emoji conversion processes occur strictly in real-time within your browser (device) and are completely destroyed as soon as the window is closed. You can safely use our tool for sensitive business emails and marketing copy.

2. Use of Local Storage

To provide a more convenient experience, the Company uses your browser's 'Local Storage'. This is strictly used only to remember the user's preferences and does not contain any tracking data that can identify an individual.

3. Web Traffic Analytics (Google Analytics)

The Company uses 'Google Analytics' to improve service quality and analyze visitor statistics. During this process, non-identifiable data such as IP address, time of visit, and operating system may be collected anonymously. Users can opt out of data collection by cookies and analytics tools at any time through their browser settings.

4. Contact Information for Privacy Matters

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our official email (info.mojitap@gmail.com) or the 'Contact Us' page.
